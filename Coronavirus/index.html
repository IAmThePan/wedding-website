Our goal is to host a Coronavirus-Responsible, Socially Distant event

Please review the New York State Travel Advisory.

Brant Lake Camp Precautions

There is ample space at Brant Lake Camp to practice Social Distancing and still have fun.

Dining Hall:

Meals will be prepared and served by masked Brant Lake staff in the Dining Hall behind a plexiglass shield.

Foot traffic in the dining hall flows in one direction with seperate doors for entering and exiting. The floor is taped with 6-foot markers to encourage social distancing while waiting in line.

All meals will be enjoyed in open air on spaced picnic tables.

Bathroom facilities in communal areas are cleaned three times each day.

Lodging:

Every individual, couple, or family will have their own bunk area and shared bathroom.

Cabins are cleaned on Tuesday and left vacant until guests arrive on Friday.

Hand Sanitizer is abundant and will be located all over camp.

Expectations

Stay home if you or anyone you've been in contact with is feeling ill.

Wear a mask in communal areas or when talking to a guest outside your "pod".

Wash your hands frequently (for at least 20 seconds, you know the drill).

Please practice safe social distancing whenever possible.

Unfortunately, we will not be hugging guests during this time.
