Charity: Eden Reforestation Projects

Eden Reforestation Projects is a charity that supports impoverished communities abroad by hiring locals to plant trees.

We like their core mission and appreciate their commitment to empowering women. Their Annual Report is a good read.

They operate in nine countries, two of which we visited on our travels: Nepal and Indonesia.
