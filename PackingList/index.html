For your Cabin

Cabins will be provided for each family, couple or individual. Each cabin has its own bathroom with toilet, toilet paper, shower and sink. Cabins have electricity but are not weatherized, it may get cold. Beds are small, and a bottom sheet, pillow and pillowcase will be provided.

Please bring your own:

  • Comforter, warm blanket or sleeping bag
  • Top sheet, if that's your style
  • Pillows & pillow cases if you are particular
  • Towels, for both showering and swimming
  • Slippers
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Toiletries: shampoo/conditioner, soap, toothpaste, ...
  • Room decor (optional)
  • Bluetooth speaker for personal use (optional)

For Activities

Saturday will be a day full of camp activities (see the schedule).

The camp has a waterfront. Swimming is encouraged. Kayaks, canoes, and stand-up paddle boards will be available for general use. You can also go fishing, rods are available.

Brant Lake is a sports camp. There are many fields and the staff will have equipment on hand for impromptu games. If you would like to bring your own game or equipment, feel free.

Please bring your own:

  • White clothing to Tie Dye (100% cotton works best)
  • Bathing suit and towel
  • Athletic clothes & shoes for playing sports
  • A musical instrument if you want to jam in the Clubhouse
  • Yoga mat
  • Sunscreen (reef safe please)
  • Lawn games or specialized equipment (SpikeBall, CamJam, baaseball mitt, etc.)
  • Hula hoop, hopscotch chalk, bubble wands, origami paper... whatever suits your fancy.

For the Wedding Festivities

Campground athletic attire is recommended for daytime and nighttime activities. The camp is on a hill so comfortable shoes are a must. Bring a hoodie for hanging around the campfire; it gets cold at night.

The ceremony will be held on a sandy, lakeside beach with a mix of rocky and paved paths. Please plan your footwear accordingly.

Please wear:

  • Dressy/Beachy Casual attire for the Ceremony
  • Flat shoes (ladies, heels are not recommended)
  • Warm sweater or shawl for cool September nights
  • A Light jacket in case of rain (optional)

For the After Party

We are putting on a Silent Disco!

A Silent Disco is an event where everyone listens to the same music on their own headphones. The music is synced so everyone hears the same thing at the same time. If you take the headphones off you will not hear a thing, hence Silent.

We think this a Covid-responsible way to have dancing. Wearing headphones will encourage solo dancing. Hearing the same music as everyone else will make dancing a communal, if distant, activity.

If you would like to participate, please bring your own:

  • Phone with the Airfoil Satellite app pre-downloaded (website)
  • Noise cancelling headphones, or other good headphones
  • Costume (encouraged)
